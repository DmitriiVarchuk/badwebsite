Skarklet

The Blood Mage of Mortal Kombat

Biography

Skarlet is a deadly warrior who serves Shao Kahn. She is made of blood magic and uses her powers to manipulate her enemies. A master of violence, Skarlet's history is bathed in war and loyalty.

Originally introduced as a DLC character, she has become one of the most iconic kombatants due to her powerful abilities and captivating backstory.

Abilities

  • Blood Ball: Skarlet throws a ball of blood energy.
  • Dagger Dance: She slashes enemies with her deadly daggers.
  • Blood Tentacle: Summons a tentacle of blood to attack enemies from afar.
